What does Agile means

Agility is not a process, it’s the ability to adapt to our complex and fast paced environment.

By working in short iterative cycles rather than widely spaced deadlines, it allows to quickly experiment and verify hypothesis.

Trust in the human capabilities to collaborate and learn together, adapt and be discipled.
